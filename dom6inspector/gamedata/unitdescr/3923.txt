Presbyter Magistrates are the highest ranking priests of the republic. They undergo extensive studies in liturgical code and theology as well as administrative and judicial code. Only when they pass rigorous exams can they be appointed by the senate. The Presbyter Magistrates rarely perform sermons, instead they oversee the performance and practice of regular Presbyters. Presbyter Magistrates wields the full authority of the republic and can collect taxes, mete out justice and lead armies. They are highly respected throughout the republic.

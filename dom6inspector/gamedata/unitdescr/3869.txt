Harbingers of the Choir are angelic beings that sing and sound their horns to celebrate the glory of the Lord. They are winged and armed with heavenly horn used in their eulogy. Harbingers are almost invulnerable to physical harm and surrounded by a radiant aura that intimidates cowardly beings. They have partial resistance to lightning and fire. Harbingers of the Choir will automatically join any arcane chorus as a chorus slave.

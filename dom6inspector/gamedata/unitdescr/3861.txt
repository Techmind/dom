With the defeat of Ermor, studies of esoterica, with or without the aid of demons, became popular. What is a mortal soul worth compared to the unimaginable riches alchemy can grant you in this life? Alchemy is the art of magical transformation. It is often used to engender wealth, but the alchemists of Marignon has also found the means to transform living flesh infusing it with alchemical elements, creating living amalgams.

There are seven Academies of High Magic. Each academy has specialized in one magic path. The academies are led by Arch Wizards, master researchers and mages of great renown. The Arch Wizards of the Hidden Flame has unlocked arcane secrets beyond the scope of mere fire magic. They have made it their task to rid the world of the Horrors of the Void and they have developed spells fueled by the Hidden Flame to aid their cause.

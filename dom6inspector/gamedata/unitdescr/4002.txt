The First Queen was once a sorceress born in Great Feminie in the Magnificent Kingdom of Ind. A master of arcana, and glamour magic in particular, she discovered the secrets of the veil that protected the kingdom. When the kingdom fell she withdrew to Feminie and recreated the veil that had protected Ind and became the first of the Sage-Queens. Hidden from the great powers of the world her might grew until she could don a godly mantle. Claiming divinity she has now revealed herself and challenge the jealous gods of the world.

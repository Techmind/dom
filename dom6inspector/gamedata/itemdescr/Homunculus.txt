The caster awakens and befriends a mandrake root by feeding it his own blood. The mandrake root is a plant most magical, endowed with sentience and a deep magical understanding. The root resembles a human and can walk around by itself once awakened. The Homunculus is fed with the blood of the caster and is bound to him and him alone. It acts as a personal servant that will aid him in research. In combat the Homunculus will aid its master with the strange power known as elf-shot.

The Dakini is a bloodthirsty apparition of vengeance from the Nether Realms. It is a sky dancer and divine messenger banished to the Nether Realms in ages past for serving as a divine acolyte of a bloodthirsty Pretender. Dakinis share their old mistress' appetites and can be summoned by the sacrifice of innocents. Their flaying daggers are enchanted to drain the life of their victims. The Dakini is a powerful Blood mage.

The caster summons one of the Great Boars of Carnutes. These magnificent beasts live in the depths of the sacred forest. They occasionally emerge from the depths of the forest to inspect the sacred grove of the Druids. Such appearances are rare and considered important omens. Its mere presence is said to prevent bad events. The Great Boar of Carnutes is the king of all boars and ordinary boars will constantly emerge from the forests to follow the Great Boar of Carnutes. It is sacred to the people of Marverni.
